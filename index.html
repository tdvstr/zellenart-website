About Zellen

Crafted in the Western Cape

Based in the scenic town of Kleinmond in the Western Cape, Zellen Art Products is a proudly South African manufacturer of fine art materials and craft supplies. We produce a comprehensive range of professional and student-grade paints, mediums, and craft products trusted by artists and retailers nationwide.

Our commitment to quality and local manufacturing means every product is carefully formulated and produced to meet the demanding standards of South African artists — from professionals working in oil and acrylic to students and crafters exploring their creativity.
